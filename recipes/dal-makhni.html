Dal Makhni

A traditional Indian/Punjabi best served with Paronthi

The most boring yet satisfying dish

Only fresh ingredients should be used

Ingredients

  • 500gm Kaali Dal
  • 100gm Rajma
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Spices
  • Red Chilli
  • Green Chilli

Recipe Steps

  1. Cook Dal until cooked
  2. Cook Rajma until cooked
  3. Tadka your Dal
  4. Mix Dal and Rajma
  5. Add spices
  6. Cook until cooked
  7. Your Dal Makhni is ready
