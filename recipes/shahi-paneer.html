Shahi Paneer

A traditional Indian best served with Butter naan

The go to dish at Restraunts

Only fresh ingredients should be used

Ingredients

  • 500gm taaja paneer
  • dhaniya
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Spices
  • Red Chilli
  • Green Chilli

Recipe Steps

  1. Cook tomatoes until cookec
  2. Add garlic and spices to tomatoes
  3. Add water until too much
  4. Blend the mixture to make gravy
  5. Add paneer to gravy
  6. Cook until cooked
  7. Your Shahi Paneer is ready
