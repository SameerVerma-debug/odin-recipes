Butter Chicken

A traditional Indian chicken dish best served with naan

The Indian dish for all the foreigners

Only fresh ingredients should be used

Ingredients

  • 500gm Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Red Chilli
  • Green Chilli

Recipe Steps

  1. Marinade chicken with spices
  2. Cook tomatoes and onions for about 5 minutes
  3. Blend all the cooked ingredients to make gravy
  4. Half tandoor your chicken
  5. Add chicken in gravy
  6. Let it cook for about 5-10 minutes
  7. Your Butter Chicken is ready
